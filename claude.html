History

Claude's Journey

2021
Anthropic founded by former OpenAI researchers Dario Amodei, Daniela Amodei, and others, focused on AI safety research.
MARCH 2023
Claude 1 launches in limited beta. Anthropic introduces Constitutional AI — a novel technique to align AI systems using a set of principles rather than pure RLHF.
JULY 2023
Claude 2 released with 100K context window — a record at the time. Significantly improved coding and reasoning capabilities.
MARCH 2024
Claude 3 family launches: Haiku, Sonnet, and Opus. Claude 3 Opus becomes the #1 model on MMLU and HumanEval benchmarks, beating GPT-4.
JUNE 2024
Claude 3.5 Sonnet launches with dramatically improved speed and coding ability. Becomes the go-to model for software engineers worldwide.
2025–2026
Claude 4 series establishes Claude as the definitive leader for coding, reasoning, and professional tasks. 200K context window. Anthropic raises $10B+ to continue scaling.
