History

Gemini's Journey

MAY 2023
Google announces Bard, powered by LaMDA/PaLM 2. Early reviews are lukewarm but Google promises rapid iteration.
DECEMBER 2023
Gemini unveiled as Google's most capable AI family: Ultra, Pro, and Nano. Debuts with a controversial demo.
FEBRUARY 2024
Gemini 1.0 Ultra launches. Bard rebranded to Gemini. Scores above human performance on MMLU for the first time.
MAY 2024
Gemini 1.5 Pro released with 1 million token context — unprecedented. Can analyze entire codebases, movies, and books.
SEPTEMBER 2024
Gemini 1.5 Flash offers ultra-fast inference at low cost. Deep integration with Google Workspace products.
2025–2026
Gemini 2.0 series launches with enhanced multimodal output, real-time reasoning, and native code execution.
