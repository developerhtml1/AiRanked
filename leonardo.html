History

Leonardo AI's Journey

OCTOBER 2022
Leonardo AI founded in Sydney, Australia. Initially focused on game asset generation for indie developers.
MARCH 2023
Public beta launches. Goes viral as 'Midjourney but for game devs.' Waitlist reaches 1 million.
JULY 2023
General access opens. Realtime Canvas and fine-tuning features launch. Raises $31M Series A.
JANUARY 2024
PhotoReal V2 achieves photorealistic quality. Phoenix model launches as flagship. Image guidance tools expanded.
JUNE 2024
Phoenix model significantly improves text within images and complex scene composition.
2025–2026
Leonardo Video launches. 3D asset generation (alpha). Partners with major game studios including Ubisoft.
