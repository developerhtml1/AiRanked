History

Midjourney's Journey

JULY 2022
Midjourney V1 launches in Discord beta. Within days, creates images indistinguishable from human art in many styles.
MARCH 2023
V4 and V5 release in rapid succession. V5 produces photorealistic portraits that fool professional photographers.
JUNE 2023
Midjourney bans free trials due to overwhelming demand. Community grows to millions of active users.
DECEMBER 2023
V6 launches with dramatically improved text rendering, coherence, and photorealism. Sets new industry standard.
MAY 2024
Midjourney V6.1 improves consistency and launches web editor with inpainting and variation controls.
2025–2026
Midjourney Video (alpha) launches. V7 achieves near-perfect photorealism. Company valued at $10B+.
