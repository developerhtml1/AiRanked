History

Mistral's Journey

APRIL 2023
Mistral AI founded in Paris by Arthur Mensch (DeepMind) and Guillaume Lample & Timothée Lacroix (Meta). Raises €105M seed.
SEPTEMBER 2023
Mistral 7B released as torrents. Outperforms Llama 2 13B. Open-source AI community goes wild.
DECEMBER 2023
Mistral 8x7B (Mixtral) MoE architecture released. Matches GPT-3.5 at fraction of cost. Valued at $2B.
FEBRUARY 2024
Mistral Large launches as API product to compete with GPT-4. Partnerships with Azure and Google Cloud.
JUNE 2024
Codestral releases as dedicated coding model. Mistral raises $640M at $6B valuation.
2025–2026
Mistral Large 3 competes with top US models. EU AI Act compliance becomes competitive advantage.
