History

Perplexity's Journey

AUGUST 2022
Perplexity AI founded with vision of replacing traditional web search with AI-powered answers.
DECEMBER 2022
Perplexity launches publicly, combining GPT-3.5 with Bing Search for cited answers.
APRIL 2023
Perplexity Pro launches with access to Claude, GPT-4, and Mistral models. Funding round closes at $25M.
