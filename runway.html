History

Runway AI's Journey

2018
Runway founded by Cristóbal Valenzuela, Alejandro Matamala, and Anastasis Germanidis while at NYU ITP.
FEBRUARY 2023
Gen-1 launches: video-to-video style transfer. Creates stylized video from existing footage.
JUNE 2023
Gen-2 releases with text-to-video generation. Runway raises $141M Series C at $1.5B valuation.
JUNE 2024
Gen-3 Alpha launches. Produces dramatically more realistic and coherent video. Hollywood studios begin licensing.
OCTOBER 2024
Gen-3 Alpha Turbo releases 5x faster. Runway raises $308M at $3B valuation.
2025–2026
Gen-4 achieves feature film quality in short clips. Samsung and Adobe partner for integration.
